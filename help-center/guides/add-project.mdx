## Overview
Let’s create a new project in Sotatek.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Go to `https://dashboard.sotatek.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Project'">
    Find the Add Project button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in project details">
    Enter the project name, description, and assign team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    Click “Create” and your project will be added.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">You’re ready to start managing your project!</Callout>
