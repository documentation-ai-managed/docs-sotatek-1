## Overview

Creating your first project in Sotatek is simple.

## Steps

1. Log in to your dashboard at `https://dashboard.sotatek.com`
2. Click "Add Project" on the top right
3. Enter your project name and details
4. Click "Create"

Your project will now appear on your project list.
