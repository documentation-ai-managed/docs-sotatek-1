---
title: Can’t log in to Sotatek
description: Steps to fix login problems.
---

## Symptoms
- Login page reloads with no error
- "Invalid credentials" message

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your email and password">
    Ensure you are using the correct credentials for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click “Forgot Password?” and follow the link sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try clearing cookies and reload the login page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">Still stuck? Email support@sotatek.com</Callout>
